1、Create a custom option group
Enable following in option group to see logs in cloudwatch.
SERVER_AUDIT_FILE_ROTATIONS 
SERVER_AUDIT_FILE_ROTATE_SIZE
2、Open the Amazon RDS console.
Choose Databases from the navigation pane.
Select the DB instance that you want to use to export log data to CloudWatch.
Choose Modify.
From the option group，select the new option group
From the Log exports section, select the Audit log.
Choose Continue.
Review the Summary of modifications, and choose Modify instance.
